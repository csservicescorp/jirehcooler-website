Lint buildup inside a dryer vent restricts airflow, forces your dryer to work harder, and is a well-documented residential fire risk. It's an easy thing to overlook until it becomes a real problem.

## Signs Your Vent Needs Cleaning

- Clothes take longer than usual to dry
- The dryer or laundry room feels unusually hot during a cycle
- You notice a burning smell while the dryer runs
- It's been over a year since your last cleaning

## A Simple Add-On Service

Many homeowners schedule dryer vent cleaning alongside their regular AC maintenance visit, keeping both systems in check on the same trip.
