Ductwork accumulates dust, debris, and buildup over time, especially in systems that haven't been serviced regularly. That buildup can affect airflow and the air circulating through your space.

## What's Involved

Our technicians inspect and clean accessible ductwork to remove buildup that's accumulated over time, checking for any damage, disconnections, or airflow restrictions along the way.

## When to Consider Duct Cleaning

- It's been several years since your ducts were last cleaned or inspected
- You've noticed inconsistent airflow between rooms
- You're addressing indoor air quality alongside a UV light or filtration upgrade
- You recently completed renovation work that may have introduced dust into the system

Ask your technician whether duct cleaning makes sense for your system during your next maintenance visit.
