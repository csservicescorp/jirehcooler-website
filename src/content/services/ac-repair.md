South Florida heat doesn't leave much room for a slow AC repair. When your system stops cooling, blows warm air, or starts making noises it shouldn't, our technicians diagnose the actual problem, not just the symptom, and give you a written estimate before any work begins.

## What We Repair

Jireh Cooler technicians work on the full range of residential and commercial air conditioning issues, including:

- Refrigerant leaks and low-charge systems
- Failed capacitors, contactors, and motors
- Compressor and condenser issues
- Frozen evaporator coils
- Electrical and control-board faults
- Airflow and ductwork restrictions

## Why Homeowners and Businesses Choose Us

We've been repairing air conditioning systems across Broward and Palm Beach County since 1994. That means we've seen how South Florida's heat and humidity wear down equipment over time, and we know how to get a system back online without cutting corners.

If a repair isn't the right long-term call for your system, we'll tell you honestly, and walk you through what a replacement would look like instead.
