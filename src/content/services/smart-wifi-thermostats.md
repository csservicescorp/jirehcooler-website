A smart or Wi-Fi thermostat gives you control over your system from your phone, whether you're adjusting the temperature before you get home or checking in on a commercial property after hours.

## What We Handle

- Compatibility check with your current AC system
- Removal of your old thermostat
- Installation and configuration of your new smart thermostat
- A walkthrough of the app and basic scheduling features

## Choosing a Thermostat

Not every smart thermostat works with every system. We'll confirm compatibility before installation day so you end up with a thermostat that actually works with the equipment you have.
