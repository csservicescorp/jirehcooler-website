UV light systems install directly into your ductwork and work continuously as air moves through your system. It's one of the more popular upgrades homeowners and businesses ask about when improving indoor air quality.

## How It Works

The UV light is mounted inside the ductwork near your indoor coil, where it operates as air circulates through your system, without requiring any change to how you use your thermostat or system day to day.

## Is It Right for Your System?

Compatibility depends on your existing ductwork and equipment. Our technicians can evaluate your system during a maintenance visit or new installation and recommend the right placement for your setup.
